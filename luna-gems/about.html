Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance since 1998

Our Journey Through Time

1998

Luna Gems began with a simple vision: to create meaningful, handcrafted jewelry that captures the essence of life’s most precious moments. What started as a small local studio grew steadily through dedication to quality and artistry.

2005

As more customers discovered the beauty and craftsmanship of Luna Gems, the brand expanded its reach through exclusive collections and a growing reputation for elegance and authenticity.

2010

Luna Gems introduced new design techniques, combining modern innovation with time-honored methods. Each piece continued to reflect a deep respect for tradition and personal storytelling.

2015

Committed to ethical practices, the brand strengthened its promise to use responsibly sourced materials and foster partnerships with trusted local artisans and suppliers.

2024

With growing demand, Luna Gems proudly opened boutiques in San Pablo City, Batangas, and Manila—bringing the timeless charm of its collections closer to home for Filipino jewelry lovers.

2025 & Beyond

Today, Luna Gems continues its journey in the Philippines with an unwavering passion for excellence, offering pieces that inspire, celebrate, and endure across generations.
